Section 03

Routing playground

transparent multi-factor scoring

Each request is scored against every eligible model on four dimensions. Org policy sets the base weights; the request's own latency and cost sensitivity reweight them, the scores renormalise, and the highest-scoring eligible model wins. Rejected alternatives and the reason are logged for audit. Pick a workload or move the sliders and watch the decision change.
